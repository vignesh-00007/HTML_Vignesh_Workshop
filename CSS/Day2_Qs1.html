Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • Bacon
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Tomato slices
  • Butter
  • Mayonnaise
  • Bread slices

Instructions:

1. Cook bacon in a skillet until crispy. Remove from skillet and drain on paper towels.

2. Butter one side of each bread slice.

3. Place a slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of each bread slice.

4. On two of the bread slices (cheese side up), add bacon slices and tomato slices.

5. Place the remaining bread slices on top (cheese side down) to form sandwiches.

6. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Place sandwiches in the skillet and cook until bread is golden brown and cheese is melted, about 2-3 minutes per side.

7. Serve hot and enjoy!
